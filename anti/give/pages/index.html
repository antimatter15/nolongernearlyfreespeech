Growth and Inspiration through Volunteering and Education (GIVE), is a community organization founded and run by high school students. The aim of this organization is to promote youth leadership through projects that improve the quality of life in their communities. We seek to invest in the next generation, by striving to provide opportunities to those who seek them in the fields of education, empowerment, and leadership. We especially emphasize the importance of learning and promote an enthusiastic attitude towards it.
